Who We Are

We are Cyber Crew. Our Name Tells Every thing about us but just in case.....

"It's still Magic even if you know how it's done !"

Cyber Crew is the computer club of Seth Anadram Jaipuria School, Ghazibad. Founded in 2014, the club helps the students to explore all things related computer. With Mithil Aggarwal being the current President and with an overwhelming assistance from the students, we at Cyber Crew, work efficiently and diligently and organize each Compufest and Melange with perfection.
We at Cyber Crew communicate using a language that only we decipher best and provide the IT fanatics with an opportunity and a platform where they take their first step towards realising their dream of deciphering computers. The best part is, we provide you with a beginning in learning IT at home, i.e our own school
