About Compufest

“The limits of the possible can only be defined by going beyond them into the impossible. The world is full of magical things patiently waiting for our wits to grow sharper.”

Success comes to those who dedicate everything to their passion in life. Determination, zeal and perseverance script a story which soars beyond the realms of a fairy tale, to inspire generations which have gone by or will live to relate. The precincts of Jaipuria, have since inception provided a promising arena to all those students who would like to unveil, contour and sharpen their innate talents and augment their potential. It is enticing all the future globe trotters to partake by providing a nurturing atmosphere to join forces, develop and foster talents and values.

“Compufest” is a Jaipurian legacy; a passionate two days extravaganza visited by multitude of young ignited superstars bubbling with palpable energy and soaring spirits. A plethora of dazzling, nerve wracking and exhilarating activities astound the young enthusiastic feisty minds, whose sheer brilliance and competitive spirits foster collaboration.

It is indeed our privilege to invite the multi-talented, dexterous scholars to participate in “Compufest-2018”-The Techno-Cultural Fest. It aims to encourage computing, cutting edge technology, creativity and ingenuity to make a quantum leap into the future. This collaborative confluence of students from different schools assists to unearth inherent aptitude, imagination and manifests intrinsic abilities. An array of divergent events ranging from Code Mash, Techno-vision, Technosway, Pot Filling Robot etc., shall provide a platform for the techno-savvy generation which needs no spotlight, however can radiate better with some impetus that shall be provided by the Jaipurian premises.

General Rules and Regulations

  • Kindly confirm your participation by 10th October 2018 by mailing us at compufest@sajs.co.in
  • Reporting time for registration of participating teams is between 8:30 am to 9:30 a.m.
  • All participating teams to report in proper school uniform & must carry their school I-card.
  • At the time of registration each school will be allotted a “School Code” that will be taken as their identity for all events.
  • One student can participate in only one event per day.
  • Class groups regarding the events are to be strictly followed.
  • For individual events specific rules & regulations are mentioned under each event.
  • Use of any unfair means is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • Host school will not participate in any of the events.
  • It is mandatory for the winning team to collect the event prizes on Day 2 i.e. 31st October 2018
  • Event details and guidelines are attached along with the brochure.
  • Kindly register for the event online by filling the form available on our website.
