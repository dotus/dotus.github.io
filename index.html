Ep. 7 - The Most Stubborn Man In India

Amidst a pandemic where most stay at home, Ronit Ranjan is walking 4000 km across India to garner support for his petition to include mental health in the coming year's curriculum. Now midway through his journey full of blisters, Ronit shares his story of growth, including how his perspectives about mental health have changed. And rest assured, yours will too.

Guest(s): Ronit Ranjan

Ep. 6 - Belarus gave her up, but she hasn't

Belarusian officials told her mother to give her up. 5000 miles away, United States welcomed her in open arms and did her surgeries for free. She is Sasha, who was caught in a house fire when she was 11 months old which resulted in severe burns across her upper body and the loss of her fingers. In this episode, we talk to her as she explains why she still fights for Belarus when Belarus gave her up.

Guest(s): Sasha
