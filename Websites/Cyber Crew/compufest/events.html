Design Your Spoon

Manual Robotics
Day 1

Rules

  • Class I-II
  • No of Participants: 3
  • Time Span: 2 Hours
  • Teams will be provided with models of eatables in different shapes and sizes.
  • Teams have to design a spoon to pick up the models of eatables from the basket and keep it onto plates.
  • Skills: Cognitive skills, on the spot decision making abilities.
  • Material Allowed: Will be provided by the host school

Judgment Criteria

  • After designing the spoon, in the second phase teams will be asked to pick up different quantities of different models into their plate in the minimum time.
  • Each model has some points and the team which earns max. points in min. time wins the competition.

Catch the fishes

Manual Robotics
Day 1

Rules

  • Class III – V
  • No of Participants: 2
  • Topic: On the Spot
  • Students will be given articles like magnet, thread and 2 pulleys (big and small). Rest of the material students can bring as per their own requirements.
  • Task is to design a fishing rod which can catch the magnetic fishes(moving & static) from the half-filled pond (total depth 30 cm)
  • Teams are not allowed to catch fishes by hands.
  • Use of electricity is not allowed.
  • Material Allowed:
    Spatuala/Icecream sticks
    Straws/pencils/sketch pens
    Measuring Tool/Bamboo sticks
    Tape/Fevicol/Thread/ Tissue papers
    Eraser/Sharpener

Judgment Criteria

Team with maximum points will win the competition.

Biz Metier

Planning Business
Day 1

Rules

  • Class: IX - XII
  • No. of Participants: 2
  • Time: 60 min.( Preliminary Round ), 10 min. ( Final Round )
  • Participants have to prepare an overall business plan in advance.
  • Each team will be given an hour to edit and make on the spot changes in their business plan according to the given situation.
  • Top 6 teams would be selected for the final round.
  • Finalists will give a five minutes presentation demonstrating their business plan.
  • They may be cross questioned accordingly by the judges and fellow participants.
  • Teams can make it lively through self-created logos and taglines.
  • Teams may carry their own laptop to showcase their business plan.

Judgment Criteria

Originality, Creativity/Innovation, Visual Appeal and presentation.
